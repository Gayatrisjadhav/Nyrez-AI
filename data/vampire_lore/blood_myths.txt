Title: Myths from the Crimson Century

- It is said the first vampire was not born, but made—in a forgotten ritual involving moonlight, silver, and the last heartbeat of a king.
- Some believe a vampire’s reflection is not lost, but *trapped*, screaming in reverse time.
- Legends tell of a bloodspring hidden beneath the ruins of Constantinople. The one who drinks it dreams for 100 years—and awakens knowing the future.
- A vampire who drinks from another vampire is said to go mad—driven by their memories and sins.
